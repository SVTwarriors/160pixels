5, Tiananmen Square Track

    5.2.2, Students Rally / Students Dispersed

   

Bonus Text:“+1 Ops Value For All 1 Ops cards”

 

 

 

 

   

Democrat Play: There are a total of seven 1 Ops cards in the 40 cards card Early Year deck. In this deck the Democrat player has two 1 Ops card he needs to play for the Democrat Event no matter what (e.g. St Nicholas Church, Helsinki Final Act) making Students Rally a slightly weaker Bonus for him than it is for his opponent. On the other hand, through the relative ease for him to obtain it, the Democrat player will more often be able to take advantage of this Bonus. If the Students Rally Bonus is in effect and the Democrat player isn't holding any good Communist TST candidate cards he can sacrifice one of his own 1 Ops events he doesn’t want to trigger on the TST for 3 Ops (or with the Reformer Memorialized Bonus, 4 Ops.) which can allow him to progress on the TST on the cheap.

Communist Play: Depending on his hand there is only one 1 Ops card the Communist player would be tempted to play for the event, Stasi. The Communist player will also want to hold either St Nicholas Church or Helsinki Final Act whenever he draws them. On all remaining Early Year 1 Ops cards the Communist Player will enjoy the Students Dispersed Bonus. If the Helsinki Final Act event hasn't been triggered some of the Democrat Early Year 1 Ops cards have events that create Support Check targets for the 2 Ops the Communist player gets because of the Students Dispersed Bonus (e.g. Jan Palach Week, Michnik , Fidesz).
