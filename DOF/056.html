Card Text: Remove 4 Communist SP, no more than 2 per space. May not be used against Dresden.

   

Democrat play: This event is the Democrat version of the State Run Media event. The event is played differently however. Because it is a Mid Year event it can often not be used to deny access to a region as by the time the event is triggered there are too many Communist SPs spread over the board. Because in Mid Year the tide of Events is turning against the Communist player the Foreign Television event can be used to keep up the pressure and force him to either trigger Democrat events he'd rather not, Play his events for Ops, or see his position crumble. Late Year this event should be held for triggering in Turn 10 AR 7, which should be worth 4 VPs in Final Scoring at the very least.

 

   

Communist play: Except in extraordinary circumstances TST or hold.
