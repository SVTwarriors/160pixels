Card Text: Eliminate 1 space in Romania. Any Democratic SPs are eliminated. Communist SPs are relocated to Bucuresti. Spaces connected to the destroyed space are now connected to each other directly. Has no effect after The Tyrant is Gone.

   

Democrat play: If the Democrat draws this card towards the beginning of Middle Year he will probably not yet have any SPs in Romania. He will be able to trigger the Systematization event to get it out of the deck and allow the Communist player to predictable eliminate either the Intellectual space or the Minority space Harghite/Covasna. Turn 7+ it should probably be TSTed unless it has been cancelled by The Tyrant is Gone ofcourse or Romania will not be scored again (Domino Theory) and Final Scoring will not be significantly effected (Presence).

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player should keep this card in the game untill he has a good target. Turn 7+ if Romanian lock down is clearly going to become critical for the Communist player's survival strategy and there is a real chance "Sajudis -> The Baltic Way -> Baltic Breakaway Republics" or “Lazlo Tokes” events will get the Democrat player access to Harghite/Covasna or (Intelligensia in the Romanian Writers space) the card can in the worst case be used anticipatory/reactively/preventatively.
