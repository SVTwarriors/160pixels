5, Tiananmen Square Track

    5.2.3, Foreign News Coverage / People’s Daily Denounces Protesters

   

Bonus Text:“Draw 3 Strategy Cards, Keep 1 Discard 2 (One Time)”

 

 

 

 

   

Democrat Play: Clearly a nice to have "One Time Use" bonus for the Democrat player, Foreign News Coverage enables him to discard Communist cards and Scoring cards he'd rather only see return Turn 7 at the earliest (unless the Communist player can use the Reformer Rehabilitated event later). (e.g. Heal Our Bleeding Wounds, ...) Because mostly the Democrat player can mitigate the effect of the Communist player's events and he by default already has quite good hand management flexibility taking this Bonus is as much about denying it to the Communist player as anything else.

 

Democrat Score:

 

   

Communist Play: A great "One Time Use" TST bonus for the Communist player IF (in most games won’t be the case) he manages to get to the TST spot before the Democrat player. The People’s Daily Denounces Protester bonus allows the Communist player (badly needed) hand management flexibility by allowing him to choose the best card of the three he draws and in most cases hold two cards at the end of the Turn. The two discarded cards will only return as a threat Turn 7 at the earliest (unless recovered from the discard pile by the Democrat player with either the Dash for the West event or the Reformer Rehabilitated event) or possibly not at all (allowing Power to be retained in a country for Final Scoring regardless). The discard also tilts what remains of the drawdeck in the Communist player's favour. If the People’s Daily Denounces Protester spot is reached in Turn 3 there is a fair chance to, discard a core Democrat card (e.g. St Nicholas Church, Helsinki Final Act, Sajudis), draw a neutral or Communist 4 Ops card, discard a recurring (and annoying) Early Year Democrat event (e.g. intelligentsia, Round Table Talks, ...), or score Poland or Hungary twice in quick succession. by Turn 4 or later the Communist player will still badly need the flexibility and discards of the People’s Daily Denounces Protester bonus to cope with the many nasty Middle Year Democrat cards. It is also the only way for the Communist player to discard a Scoring Card albeit relying on the chance of the draw. Thereby he could retain power in countries longer effecting the Domino Theory event and New Year’s Eve Party event, keeping the VP track below zero effecting the 40th Year Anniversary Celebration event, granting the Communist player the time to improve his position in the country and depending on the Turn 7 to Turn 10 draws retain a country for Final Scoring. If early on in the game the Communist player is doing well on the TST he should start doubling down there. This means TSTing higher Ops Democrat cards lower down on his TST candidate list to have higher chance to advance and triggering the Li Peng event as soon as possble. This (further) primes the Reformer Rehabilitated event and gives the Communist player more chances to TST roll his way past the problematic 8 Students Arrested spot in a timely fashion. This in turn allows him to enjoy the later TST Bonuses for longer and the possibility of the The Chinese Solution event triggering. All of the above makes this TST Bonus better for the Communist player as it is for the Democrat player.
