Card Text: Place 3 Democratic SPs in the Polish Intellectuals space.

   

Democrat play: Weighing the Ops against the Event the Democrat player will clearly be tempted to play the Michnik card as Event over the 1 Ops. This is not always the right move however. Things to consider are, will the event effect the outcome of the Poland Scoring event (imminent or upcoming)?, Do you get +1 Ops from an Event or the Students Rally TST spot?, Has Helsinki Final Act already been triggered? Is the Legacy of Martial Law event still a “risk”? Once the Democrat player has Power in Poland it should be played for the 1 Ops unless Late Year the extra space wrestles away Poland Domination from the Communist player for Final Scoring.

 

   

Communist play: If the Democrat player already has Power in Poland and/or already has SPs in the “Polish Writers” space through the Intelligentsia event for example then the Michnik event isn’t really a problem for the Communist player. The Democrat player’s position for Final Scoring is likely only to improve once Poland falls resulting in at least the Democrat player’s Domination by Final Scoring regardless of the Michnik event. Else if he needed to the Democrat player would still be able to move into the “Polish Writers” space with Ops under the cover of the Helsinki Final Act event or through the recurring Intelligentsia event. If however the Communist player wants to fight to retain Power in Poland then he shouldn’t play Michnik for a measly 1 Ops. The Michnik event gives the Democrat player control of an extra (overprotected intellectuals) space in Poland and allows him to quite easily play into the “Jagellonian University” space. This gives the Democrat at least 4 extra Power Struggle cards and activates a total of 5 Leaders cards in the 52 card Power Struggle deck making the Power Struggle harder to win. Sometimes the Communist player will not be able to hold or TST the Michnik card however. If the Communist player needs to trigger the Michnik event he should play it before the Helsinki Final Act event so he can use the Ops to Support Check the “Polish Writers” space without a VP penalty. Two 1 Ops Support Checks on a 2 Stability Space at a +0 modifier roll will on average remove only 2 SP, not enough to mitigate the effect of the Event. With some luck or the Students Dispersed TST spot or the Perestroika event the Communist player would on average remove 3.333 SPs which wouldn’t enable the Democrat player to Support Check back.
