Card Text: Place 1 Democratic SP in three spaces in East Germany.

   

Democrat play: If Helsinki Final Act has been triggered and the Democrat player hasn't yet found his way into the East German Student spaces Intellectual 2 SPs of the New Forum event can be used for this. The last Ops can be placed in Erfurt or be used to overprotect the Lutheran Church / Leipzig space if East Germany is dominated by the Communist player. Make sure you don't give the Communist player Support Check targets with high modifiers when placing your SPs. It possibly isn't worth playing it for the 1 Ops to keep the card game.

 

   

Communist play: If the Communist player wants to push ahead on the TST he cannot afford to be TSTing low Ops cards like New Forum, if however the TST isn't a of a higher priority as the strategic importance of East Germany at this point in the game New Forum can certainly be TSTed. So, if for example the Democrat player has already taken Power in East Germany and most battlegrounds are already under his control with a Communist presence limited to Dresden the card can be played for the 1 Ops. If however the Democrat player hasn't yet gotten access to the Student and Intellectual spaces in East Germany protected by Helsinki Final Act and the Communist player is seriously on top and invested in East Germany Power it is probably better to TST as opposed to spend alot of Ops overprotecting to not give the Democrat opportunities to irreparably damage the Communist players East Germany position.
