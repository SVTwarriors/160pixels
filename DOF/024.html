Chain of Events: St. Nicholas Church → The Monday Demonstrations

 

Canceled by / No effect after Resolution of: None

 

  
Card Text: Place sufficient Democratic SPs in the Lutheran Church space for Democratic control. Allows play of The Monday Demonstrations.

   

Democrat play: If the Democrat player draws the St. Nicholas Church card he should trigger it for the event in a slow Action Round. This event is the prerequisite for the mind bogglingly powerful The Monday Demonstrations event. It also gives the Democrat player a safe space in East German as long as the The Monday Demonstrations event could still trigger and gives the Democrat player SPs to move if the “We are the people!” event is triggered. Early Year and Middle Year when the Communist is holding on to the a href=024.html>St. Nicholas Church card it is often worth triggering the St. Nicholas Church event from his hand with the Kiss of Death event or the Goodbye Lenin! event or from the Discard pile with the Dash for the West event or the Reformer Rehabilitated event.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player should do just about everything in his power not to trigger the St. Nicholas Church event until East Germany is completely lost anyway or there is no way the The Monday Demonstrations event can still trigger. The St. Nicholas Church event is also best not triggered before the “We are the people!” event unless the Democrat player has already filled up the “Lutheran Church” space with SPs by using Ops. The Communist player not wanting to have the The Monday Demonstrations event trigger also means holding it against either the Dash for the West event and the Reformer Rehabilitated event or discarding it against the Goodbye Lenin! event and the Kiss of Death event.
