4, Access denial and Lockout

    4.1.5, Romania

 

   

Because Romania scoring is only a Late Year card and Mid Year Communist events like Ceausescu and Systematization can punish any early move by the Democrat player into Romania the Communist player has a lot more time to secure Romania compared to the other countries. The “free” SPs of the Democrat triggering Romania/Balkan specific events like Cult of Personality and Nepotism can allow the Communist to build up his position without having to sacrifice too many Ops. The Early Year event Cult of Personality is very important in this regard as it will often get triggered Early Year and form the foundation of the Communist player’s Romania strategy and will therefore be the focus here.

Typical chain of events:
1, (Democrat player) Cult of Personality trigger

2, (Communist player) Secure South East Romania - counters a,

3, (Democrat player) Ruse trigger and Communist player reaction - counters a,

4, (Communist player) Secure North West Romania b & c

5, (Democrat player / Communist player) Systematization - c
6, (Communist player) Secure Eastern worker spaces – d
