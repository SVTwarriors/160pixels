Card Text: If you are ahead on the Tiananmen Square Track, draw any non-Scoring card form the discard pile. The Event takes place immediately.

   

Democrat play: Because of the Democrat player's lower required roll to advance on the TST at the start of the game when Reformer Rehabilitated is drawn the Democrat player will often be ahead and therefore be able to search the discard for a non-Scoring card to trigger immediately. There usually should be something worth triggering as the Communist player TSTs and discards strong Democrat events he cannot hold. These events can be cards that are part of an event chain (e.g. Sadjudis, St. Nicholas Church, ...) or powerful cards in their own right (e.g. Gorbachev Charms the West, Legacy of 1968, Troop Withdrawals, ...). As the game progresses the Communist player will likely catch up to the Democrat player on the TST and the Reformer Rehabilitated event will only be useful for him so if there is nothing worth triggering in the discard pile it could be a good idea to hold on to Reformer Rehabilitated untill next Turn. Also the Democrat player shouldn't reveal he is holding Reformer Rehabilitated by nonchalantly looking through the Discard pile only when he is considering to play Dash for the West, Reformer Rehabilitated or Domino Theory. A good player should regularly browse the Discard pile to throw his opponent off the scent.

 

   

Communist play: If the Communist player draws the Reformer at the start of Middle Year he is more likely to be behind on TST and therefore play it as Ops hoping it will come back to him Late Year when he is ahead. Once Reformer Rehabilitated and Dash for the West are no Longer an immediate threat the Communist player has to reassess if holding a problematic Democrat event card is a better strategy than discarding it considering Kiss of Death, Goodbye Lenin! and Presidential Visit.
