Card Text: Place in front of the Communist Player. In his next Support Check roll in a Student space he receives a +1 modifier, then remove this card from the game.

   

Democrat play: The Democrat player should always play the Tear Gas card for the 1 Ops triggering this weak Event. Whenever possible, the Democrat player should try to play the Tear Gas card with a +1 (or more) Ops modifier either from a Democrat event or the Students Rally TST bonus.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player should always play the Tear Gas card for the 1 Ops letting the Democrat Player trigger the Tear Gas event for him later. The event is weak but the Communist player should not lose track of the Tear Gas event in front of him once it has been triggered. The Communist player should try to play the Tear Gas card for 2 Ops through reaching the Students Dispersed TST spot first if possible. Else the Perestroika event can help to increase the Tear Gas card’s Ops value.
