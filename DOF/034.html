Card Text: Place 5 Democratic SPs in the Hungary Student space.

   

Democrat play: After the Hungary Scoring card is removed form the game the Fidesz card should nearly always be played for the 2 Ops. This is also the case prior to the Helsinki Final Act event is active. Only in very rare situations like Hungary access denial by the Communist player or a very close struggle to gain the upper hand in Hungary is this not the case.

 

   

Communist play: The 2 Ops of the Fidesz card (as opposed to the 1 Ops of the Michnik card and the Jan Palach Week card) and the fact the Democrat player usually already has direct access to the “Eotvos Lorand University” space through his initial SP in Budapest make the Fidesz event, relatively speaking, easier to trigger for the Communist player. If the Budapest space is still contested the Communist player should be careful not to tip a balance of power of Support Checks through the Control of neighboring spaces If the Communist player has already wrestled away control of Budapest space from the Democrat player and the Helsinki Final Act event isn’t in effect yet the 2 Ops of the Fidesz card can simply be used for a Support Check with a +1 Modifier on the “Eotvos Lorand University” space. upcoming and imminent triggering of the Hungary scoring event should of course always be considered. Once the Democrat player has taken Power in Hungary the Fidesz card is mostly a free 2 Ops fro the Communist player.
