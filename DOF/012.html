Card Text: Place 3 Communist SPs in any Bureaucratic space(s).

   

Democrat play: It is not advisable for the Democrat player to trigger the Apparatchiks event in Early Year as the 2 Ops doesn't make up for the 3 SPs the Communist player gets to place in (a) Bureaucratic space(s) allowing him to take control of these key Battlegrounds early on and thereby giving him a leg up in locking down these countries. Therefore in Early Year this card should be held or TSTed by the Democrat player. As the game progresses the Communist player will already be in control of most Bureaucratic spaces and the Apparatchiks event's SPs will be used to overprotect Bureaucratic spaces already controlled by the Communist player or take control of the last remaining uncontrolled Bureaucratic spaces making the Apparatchiks event more predictable and less threatening.

 

   

Communist play: Before Turn 7 the Communist player shouldn’t trigger the Apparatchiks event himself and play the Apparatchiks card for it’s 2 Ops to try to have it return as a “problem” (albeit a minor one) in the Democrat player’s hand. The Democrat player will then about half of the time trigger it and allow the Communist player to place the three SPs. The SPs placed into each Bureaucratic space of the different countries has different effects. Placing 1 SP in the “Praha” space improves the chance of a successful Support Check roll on the “Charles University” space (e.g. the Jan Palach Week event), while placing 1 SP in Berlin is done in anticipation or as a reaction to a move into the “Leipzig” space from the “Lutheran Church” space or the “Walter Ulbricht Academy” space from the “German Writers” space by the Democrat player in an attempt to spread into the rest of East Germany. Taking control of the “Warsawa” space with 2 SPs can improve the Communist player’s chance of a successful Support Check roll on the “Gdansk” space or the “Lodz” space, help him build up a stable foothold in the East of Poland or simply get 2 extra Power Struggle cards for the Poland Scoring event. If the Communist player is feeling confident and has a lot of Ops to spare 3 SPs in Budapest can be used to wrestle away control of Budapest from the Democrat player while watching out for the Hungarian Democratic Forum event. Taking Control of the “Sofia” space with 1 SP Early Year can help with a Turn 4 scoring of Bulgaria or contain the Democrat in the Bulgarian Intellectual space and Student space. trengthening the Communist player’s position for an "early" Middle Year Bulgaria scoring. While controlling the “Bucaresti” space covers the “Constanta” space which in turn covers the “Varna” space in the Bulgarian worker triangle.
