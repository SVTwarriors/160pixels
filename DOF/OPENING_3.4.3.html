3, Communist player’s Opening

    3.4.3, Poland Defensive Opening (Split Opening)

 

   

The Communist player doesn't try to Support Check on Turn 1 but instead opens with SP placement or an event to attempt to gain an early foothold in both Poland and Hungary. Later based on the events in Turn 1 and possibly the cards he draws Turn 2 he can decide to focus on one of the two Early Year countries.
