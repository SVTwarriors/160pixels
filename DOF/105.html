Card Text: Place 2 Democratic SPs each in Kosice and Presov. Make one Support Check with a +2 modifier in Bratislava using the Ops value of this card.

   

Democrat play: The Brno-Ostrava-Bratislava worker triangle is probably already under Democrat Control by the time Public Against Violence is drawn making the event pretty weak at this stage of the game. Ifsomehow it isn't Late Year events Public Against Violence and Civic Forum will give the Democrat another shot to break it open.

 

   

Communist play: If the Democrat player has already taken Power in Czechoslovakia and he is in control of the Brno-Ostrava-Bratislava worker triangle which will often be the case by Late Year there is nothing holding the Communist player back from triggering the event for 3 Ops. The 4 Ops in the farmer spaces probably won't effect Final scoring as the Democrat player often already has Domination or even Control and they aren't Battlegrounds. If Early Year the Communist player did however manage to get the upper hand in the Brno-Ostrava-Bratislava worker triangle depending on the modifier he should TST it.
