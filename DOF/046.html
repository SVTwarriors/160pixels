Card Text: The Communist Player must reveal all his Events with title written in red. The Democrat may take any one of them and play it for the Event, with the Event occurring immediately, or he may play this card for "Operations.

   

Democrat play: The Goodbye Lenin! event is a multipurpose and flexible event for the Democrat player. The Goodbye Lenin! event can be used by the Democrat player if he knows the Communist player is holding a red event that the Democrat player wants to trigger (e.g. the St. Nicholas Church event, the Helsinki Final Act event) with the Communist player’s Hand Size Reduction as a Bonus. This bonus Hand Size Reduction is particularly painful for the Communist player if he has triggered the Common European Home or discarded a card on the Inflationary Currency event earlier that Turn or the Presidential Visit event was triggered the previous Turn. It can also be used in combination with Kiss of Death. But the Democrat player must remember the Communist player could still use the Honecker event or the One-time TST Bonus of the People’s Daily Denounces Protesters spot to increase his hand size. The Communist player holding red events does not oblige the Democrat player to trigger them however. The Democrat can gain information about the Communist players hand strength and still use the Goodbye Lenin! event for 3 Ops. It is of course best to play the Goodbye Lenin! card while under a +1 Modifier event like the The Sinatra Doctrine event. When under a -1 Modifier event like the Prudence event the Democrat should always trigger the red events with the Goodbye Lenin! event. Before the Turn 7 reshuffle the Goodbye Lenin! card could in theory simply be played for the 3 Ops, thereby keeping it in the deck, but even then triggering the event seems the wiser choice for the Democrat player.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player doesn’t like drawing the Goodbye Lenin! card. The Communist player really can’t really win here unless he isn’t holding a red card and is able to give the Democrat player a -1 Modifier. Normally either he discards all his red cards, gets 3 Ops and gives the Democrat 3 Ops or he gets 3 Ops and lets the Democrat player choose between the red card(s) and 3 Ops. Therefore the Goodbye Lenin! card will often be discarded or held. If the Democrat player is unable to trigger the Reformer Rehabilitated event or the Dash for the West event successfully while the Goodbye Lenin! card is still in the deck, the Communist player should discard the red cards if he doesn't want to have them triggered instead of holding them.
