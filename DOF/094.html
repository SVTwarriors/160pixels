Card Text: Remove 4 Communist SPs from Bulgaria, no more than 2 per space. Then make 2 Support Checks in Bulgaria using the Ops value of this card.

   

Democrat play: The Union of Democratic Forces event is another powerful Democratic 4 Ops event. It can change Bulgaria from Communist stronghold to a Communist headache probably making it one of the best cards for Turn 10 Action Round 7 if Bulgaria is colored red.

 

   

Communist play: This will always be a TST unless something major has already gone wrong for the Communist player in Bulgaria and there are 4 or less Communist SPs to remove. This overkill would waste the event's SC and force the Democrat to remove possible future support check targets. Else it is like so often for the Communist player in Late Year damage control.
