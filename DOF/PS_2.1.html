2, Power Struggle

    2.1, Introduction

 

   

In this Chapter the different aspects of the Power Struggle will be discussed. First the distribution and different card types and the so-called suits of Power Struggle Cards will be explained here with the broad approaches to play each. Then the Strategy cards with events effecting Power Struggles are discussed here. Then the importance of Presence for Power Struggles is stressed here. Finally some tactics and bluff that can be used to win Power Struggles are summed up here.
