Card Text: +1 modifier for Democrat Support Checks in Eastern Europe for the rest of this turn.

   

Democrat play: This will usually be played for the 3 Ops by the Democrat player unless he plans to play a lot of cards as Ops in Eastern Europe. This would occur be more likely towards the beginning of Middle Year when many Czechoslovakia spaces are still up for grabs or if the Democrat player managed to wiggle his way into East Germany. Poland and Hungary will often already be built up or the Democrat will already have taken Power. Later in Middle Year the FRG Embassies event could still be interesting to secure East Germany after a successful The Monday Demonstrations event or following sustained pressure. Please not the Communist could pressure you in Bulgaria in retaliation. If you have -1 Ops from Prudence the FRG Embassies event is probably better than the 2 Ops.

 

   

Communist play: If the Communist player can play this event on his last Action Round he can minimize it’s impact He should easily be able to find somewhere to spend these 3 Ops relatively painlessly.
