Card Text: Place 1 Democratic SP in Timisoara and Harghita/Covasna. The Democratic player may them place SPs or make Support Check rolls in Romania using the Ops value of this card. Allows play of Massacre in Timisoara.

   

Democrat play: A relatively weak Democrat event that isn’t worth triggering unless the Democrat player is really desperate to get access to the North-East of Romania through Harghite/Covasna (if it hasn’t been eliminated by Systematization) and the Sajudis -> The Baltic Way -> Breakaway Baltic Republics event chain or the Toxic Waste event aren’t possible/likely. Normally it should be played for the 2 Ops. This way you also don’t need to worry about Massacre in Timisoara later on.

 

   

Communist play: Often the Democrat will already have SPs in Harghite/Covasna though the Sajudis -> The Baltic Way -> Breakaway Baltic Republics event chain and has access to Timisoara through presence in Szeged. Therefore this card will often be played for the 2 Ops by the Communist player triggering the event. This also allow play of Massacre in Timisoara later on.
