Card Text: Roll a die. If the die roll exceeds the number of Democratically controlled spaces in East Germany, then place 4 Communist SPs in East Germany, no more than 2 per space. This event prevents play of Honecker for the event.

   

Democrat play: By Late Year usually the Democrat should have at least 3 spaces (Intellectual, Student & Church) powerful East Germany Middle Year event's like The Monday Demonstrations, “We are the people!” and Democrat events that keep up the pressure like Genscher, Soviet Troop Withdrawals, ... should have the Democrat player controlling way more spaces by then. Therefore this event will often be easy to trigger. In the rare event the Democrat player's position in East Germany is fragile when he would have to trigger this event he could consider TSTing it.

 

   

Communist play: Very rarely will this event be worth triggering by the Communist player. If only it were a Middle Year card...
