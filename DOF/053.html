Card Text: +1 modifier to all Communist Tiananmen Square Track attempt for the rest of the game.

   

Democrat play: Late Year and after the Communist player has reached the Structure Removed TST spot the Democrat player can consider triggering the Li Peng event for 2 Ops as there aren’t that many Turns left for the Communist player to benefit from the Li Peng event and the Communist player should already be able to easily advance on the TST from the Structure Removed TST spot onwards anyway. The triggering of the Li Peng event by the Democrat player still depends on where exactly the Communist player is on the TST in relation to the Democrat player as to limit the number of turns the Communist player can enjoy the TST bonuses. The Democrat player should keep track of where the The Chinese Solution card is and how much triggering the Li Peng event will help trigger the The Chinese Solution event. Otherwise the Li Peng card should be TSTed by the Democrat player.

 

   

Communist play: The Li Peng event can help make an already good run on the TST spectacular. If the Communist player has a good chance to get to the Protesters massacred before the The Chinese Solution card is drawn it should certainly be played for the event. If the Communist player is behind on the TST and there are enough Turns left in the game to make it worth his while the Li Peng event should also be triggered. So except sometimes in Late Year near the end of the game the Li Peng event should always be triggered by the Communist player.
