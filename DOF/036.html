Canceled by / No effect after Resolution of: None

 

  
Card Text: Roll a die. If the die roll exceeds the number of Communist controlled spaces in East Germany, +1 VP and select any Democratic Event with an asterik(*) from the discard pile. The event on that card occurs immediately.

   

Democrat play: Some games by Turn 3 the Communist player will start building up his position in East Germany to withstand the onslaught of the many upcoming Middle Year and Late Year Democrat events. When this build up is complete a successful roll on the Dash for the West event becomes difficult until the Communist player’s position is weakened later in the game. If Turn 3 there are (significant) asterisked Democrat cards in the discard pile that are crucial to the Democrat player’s strategy (e.g. the St. Nicholas Church card, the Helsinki Final Act card, the Eco-Glasnost card, the Walesa card …) and a successful roll on the Dash for the West event is still possible it should be considered.

 

   

Communist play: Turn 1 Action Round 1 and Turn 3 Action Round 1 an empty discard pile due to the reshuffle allows the Communist player to play the Dash for the West card for 3 Ops with (up to) a +1 VP penalty. Otherwise if there are (significant) asterisked Democrat cards in the discard pile the Communist player should control enough East German Spaces to make a successful Democrat roll on the Dash for the West event unlikely or hold or TST the Dash for the West card where possible. This is best done without looking through the discard pile so the Communist player doesn’t give away he’s holding Dash for the West card.
