4, Access denial and Lockout

    4.1.2, Czechoslovakia

 

   

In most games Czechoslovakia is a slam dunk for the Democrat player. In some games however the Communist player can gain the upper hand in the Brno-Ostrava-Bratislava worker triangle through access denial by removing the 1 SP from the Czechoslovakian Catholic Church space and if necessary from Katowice with either the State Run Media or Gorbachev Charms the West event. This should allow the Communist to reach the Czechoslovakian Catholic Church first and/or consolidate Brno-Ostrava-Bratislava worker triangle. It is expensive though and will probably require sacrificing other countries.

Last 2 Communist player intial influence SPs: The Communist places 1 Initial influence SP in Praha and 1 Initial influence SP in Brno
Step 1: The Communist plays State Run Media or Gorbachev Charms the West and removes ALL Democrat SPs from the Czechoslovakian Catholic Church space and removes ALL Democrat SPs from Katowice.

Step 2: The Democrat player retakes Katowice
Step 3: The Communist player takes Brno with 1 SP and places 3 SP in Bratislava or Ostrava

Alternatively if the Communist player does not have enough Ops to take Brno & Ostrava or Brno & Bratislava outright Last 2 Communist player intial influence SPs: The Communist places 1 Initial influence SP in Praha and 1 Initial influence SP in Brno
Step 1: The Communist plays State Run Media or Gorbachev
Charms the West and removes ALL Democrat SPs from the Czechoslovakian Catholic Church space and removes ALL Democrat SPs from Katowice.
Step 2: The Democrat player retakes Katowice
Step 3: The Communist player takes Brno and places 1 SP in Bratislava
Step 4: The Democrat player moves into Ostrava
Step 5: The Communist player is the first to place SPs in the Czechoslovakian Catholic Church space

If the Communist player wants to get to the Catholic Church spaces sooner to avoid a bidding war he can place Initial influence 1 SP in Praha and 1 SP in Presov instead of Brno.

Cards the Democrat player can use to counter this move are Papal visit, Consumerism, Gorbachev Charms the West and/or enough Ops to come out on top in a struggle for the Czechoslovakian Catholic Church space.
