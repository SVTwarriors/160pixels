Card Text: Place in front of the Democratic Player. The Democrat receives a +1 modifier to the Support Loss and Victory Point die rolls if he wins the next Power Struggle. Then remove this card from the game.

   

Democrat play: When the Democrat is desperate to take power in a country of an upcoming Power Struggle and his position for the Power Struggle cannot be improved with the 2 Ops he can play Yakovlev Counsels Gorbachev for the event. This can allow him not to discard 3 potentially useful PS cards for a +1 modifier, allow him to still have a 50% chance of taking power on a Petition win with a 3 PS card discard, or a 83% chance of taking power on a march/strike win with a 3 PS card discard. Unless going into Turn 7 it is clear this country will likely be scored next it is probably better for the Democrat to play for Ops.

 

   

Communist play: An easy event to trigger if Late Year no more countries will be scored, the Communist player is confident the next country scored he will surrender Power anyway or it will be a completely hopeless PS for the Democrat player. The bigger the chance the Democrat player wins the next PS in a contested country the more you will need to rely on his roll to retain Power and the less the Communist player will want to trigger the Yakovlev Counsels Gorbachev. But considering all the worse Democrat events in his hand he will often still have to play this for the event.
