5, Tiananmen Square Track

    5.2.5, Goddess of Democracy / Structure Removed

   

Bonus Text:"Discard Non-Power Struggle Card Draw Replacement Card At Start of Turn" (Please note: After the draw phase at the start of the Turn.)

 

 

 

 

   

Democrat Play: A good bonus for the Democrat player that, at the start of the Turn, further facilitates his already easy hand management. If the Democrat player is able to get to the Goddess of Democracy spot early on he will likely enjoy this Bonus multiple Turns as the Communist player tries to get to the Structure Removed spot, requiring a whopping 8 to advance. Often the Goddess of Democracy spot is also where the Democrat player's TST advance starts to slow as his requirement to advance on the TST continues to increase.

 

Democrat Score:

 

   

Communist Play: Because Middle Year and Late Year there are so many problematic (and some near fatal) Democrat events for the Communist player, he will always be able use the Structure Removed bonus to great effect. I will give him the much needed breathing room to manage his hand and limit the damage of the remaining cards. An added bonus of advancing to the Structure Removed spot is getting over the 8 hump and gaining access to spots beyond with their powerful and ever easier to obtain bonuses that lie downhill.
