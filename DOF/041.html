Canceled by / No effect after Resolution of: The Tyrant is Gone

 

  
Card Text: Remove 3 Democratic SPs in Romania and make a Support Check in Romania using the Ops value of this card. If the Democratic Player has an SP in a space adjacent to Cluj-Mapoca at the end of this Action Round remove 1 Communist SP from Bucuresti. Has no effect after The Tyrant is Gone.

   

Democrat play: In the first half of Middle Year except if the The Baltic Way event or the Lazlo Tokes event has been triggered first the Ceausescu event will mostly have no effect as there won’t be any Democrat SPs in Romania to remove or Support Check and the Democrat player can easily trigger the Ceausescu event for 3 Ops. Towards the end of Middle Year the Democrat player will be trying to move into Romania with SP placement with Ops and events (e.g. the Toxic Waste event, the Lazlo Tokes event, …) and his position might be vulnerable to the Ceausescu event making the Ceausescu card a valid TST candidate. Late Year the Democrat player shouldn’t count on the The Tyrant is Gone event to neutralize this event as it only triggers after Romania scoring by when it is too late to matter apart from Final Scoring or a very rare second scoring of Romania with the Domino Theory event or the New Year’s Eve Party event.

 

   

Communist play: If the Communist player puts up a fight in East Germany by building it up there are two ways for the Democrat to gain the upper hand in East Germany. The first revolves around the The Monday Demonstrations event if the Communist player hasn’t been able to prevent the pre-requisite St. Nicholas Church event from triggering. The The Monday Demonstrations event is even more brutal if it can be triggered with a +1-2 Ops modifier combo from a Democrat event(s) (e.g. The Sinatra Doctrine event, the FRG embassies event) while avoiding a -1-3 Ops modifier from a Communist and/or Neutral event(s) (e.g. the Prudence event, the Grenztruppen event, the Stand Fast event. The second is the breaking of Communist controlled spaces with the help of Democrat events and through superior Ops eroding the Communist player’s position until it can no longer be held and it collapses. Moving into and taking control of the “Leipzig” space is of particular interest here as it can no longer be Support Checked by the Communist player after the triggering of the ”We are the People!” event. Together with Democrat player control of the “Walter Ulbricht University” space which will often be protected by the Helsinki Final Act event threaten the “Berlin” space. If neither of these options are possible or succeed the Democrat player will need to discard the the East Germany Scoring card where possible and choosing the path of least resistance focus on the other countries on the map.
