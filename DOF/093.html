Card Text: Choose any country where the Democratic Player has Power. Roll a die. Schock Therapy is succesful 3-6, -1 modifier for every Communist controlled Worker or Farmer space in the country. If succesful, +3 VPs and place 3 Democratic SPs in the country.

   

Democrat play: By Late Year the Democrat should have at least one country where he Holds power where he is squeezing the Communist player. If in this country he can get a good roll modifier for the Shock Therapy event (e.g. 3-6 or 4-6) he should trigger it. This way he has a good chance for the 3 VPs and forcing the Communist player to repair the damage to his non-overprotected spaces in that country or risking Non-presence or Democrat Domination for Final Scoring.

 

   

Communist play: If there is a country where the Democrat Holds Power and there are still uncontrolled Worker or Farmer spaces you can access it is worth playing into them to reduce the chance of a successful Democrat roll. If this isn’t possible and the Democrat would get a good roll it is worth TSTing.
