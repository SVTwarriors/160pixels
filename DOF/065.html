Card Text: Communist hand size is reduced to 7 next turn.

   

Democrat play: For the Democrat this card will normally be player for 3 Ops but it can be used Turn 6 if you suspect or know the Communist player is holding on to a good Democrat event and there are still a lot of good Democratic Middle Year events to be drawn from what is left of the Deck. This way, next Turn, the Communist player cannot hold on to any cards (unless TSTs People's Daily Denounces Protesters can still be used) and he will only be able to discard one problematic event without sacrificing an Action Round on the TST (unless TSTs Structure Removed) or be forced to Sacrifice an Action Round with Common European Home. It combines can also combines well with Kiss of Death, Goodbye Lenin! and Inflationary Currency.

 

   

Communist play: Considering the other Democrat events that might be in the Communist players hand this is one that he might want to trigger for the 3 Ops. If the Communist player decides to do so he should TST the worst Democrat event and where possible try to mitigate the rest. It is probably a good idea to hold one good card this Turn if possible. (or 2 with Honecker or TSTs People's Daily Denounces Protesters) This way there is less of a chance you've painted yourself in a corner next Turn.
