Canceled by / No effect after Resolution of: None

 

  
Card Text: Pick any Worker space in a country your opponent has Power. Roll a die. Revolt successful 4-6. If successful replace all opponent's SPs with your own. -1 modifier for every opponent controlled adjacent space.

   

Democrat play: If by chance the Communist controls an (relatively) isolated worker space in a country he controls normally after a Democratic event and/or continued pressure it is worth playing the Workers revolt event. Otherwise, doing a quick calculation, (chance of successful roll*SP effect of successful roll) he will often conclude it is better to play it for the 2 Ops.

 

   

Communist play: The Workers revolt event is way more useful for the Democrat player as it is for the Communist player because it can only be played in a country "where your opponent holds Power". Once the Democrat player has taken Power in a country, the country becomes less important strategically and he will often have a good die roll modifier against this event, so play for 2 Ops.
