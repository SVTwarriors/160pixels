Card Text: Roll a die. On a 3-6 add 4 Communist SPs in Czechoslovakia, no more than 2 per space. -1 modifier for every Worker space in Czechoslovakia that is controlled by the Democratic Player.

   

Democrat play: By Late Year the Democrat player should control the Brno-Ostrava-Bratislava worker triangle through a myriad of Democrat events. Late Year with the Civic Forum and Public Against Violence events this is nearly guaranteed. With 3 worker spaces under his control the Communist player needs to roll a 6 for success. Even though this roll is unlikely the event should still be TSTed when possible. In some situations involving the Civic Forum event when TST isn’t possible the Democrat could try to push for control of Ceske Budejovice but mostly it won’t be worth the effort and the Democrat will just have to take a chance.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player will normally not trigger this event and play for 2 Ops instead because the Democrat will be in control of 3 worker spaces resulting in an unfavorable odds. Even if the Communist player were to roll successfully often the situation in Czechoslovakia is so dire it won’t really effect Czechoslovakia or Final Scoring by much.
