Card Text: +1 VP. The democrat chooses any country except Romania. The Communist cannot make Support Checks in this country for the rest of the turn.

   

Democrat play: If the Democrat player draws the scoring card of a country he cannot discard the Foreign Currency Debt Burden event can allow Democrat player to play into Student spaces and Intellectual spaces that aren’t protected by the Helsinki Final Act event. It also works very well together with the Genscher event in East Germany enabling to move into and take over spaces with superior Ops without fear of retaliation. Also It can neutralize the triggering of events with inflexible Support Check target countries, in Romania (e.g. the Ceausescu event and the Massacre in Timisoara event), in Bulgaria (the Politburo Intrigue event), in Poland (the Legacy of Martial Law event), he Communist player triggering a scoring card with the Kiss of Death event or the Kremlin Coup! event. Overall triggering this event is better for the Democrat player than it’s standard 1 Ops. The Communist player will likely react to the triggering of the event with the beefing up of his overprotected spaces in the chosen country.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player should of course trigger the Foreign Currency Debt Burden event for 1 Ops in Action Round 7 or 8 where possible. This way the event is unlikely to have an effect except for the +1VP. If Turn 7 isn’t possible it is best triggered as close to the end of the Turn as possible as the Foreign Currency Debt Burden event can cobine well with other Democrat event to weaken the foundation of a Communists stronghold making it collapse like a house of cards.
