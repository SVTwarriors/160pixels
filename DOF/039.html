Card Text: Place 4 Democratic SPs in Ruse. +1 VP for Communist Support Checks in Ruse for the rest of the game.

   

Democrat play: Early Year the Democrat player should use the Eco-Glasnost event to gain access to (and gain the upper hand in) the Bulgarian worker triangle (Ruse-Varna-Burgas) if he hasn’t already through the Sajudis event in an Action Round and before the Communist player gains the upper hand in the Bulgarian worker triangle. On the one hand having presence in the “Razgrad” through triggering the Sajudis event can (in combination the Eco-Glasnost event’s “+1 VP” event text) help protect the Eco-Glasnost event’s SPs in the “Ruse” space against Support Checks by the Communist player on the other hand it can redirect the Communist player’s attention to the important Bulgarian worker triangle and allow him to build up his position there in response.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player should first gain the upper hand in the Bulgarian worker triangle and only then trigger the Eco-Glasnost event. When after the triggering of the Eco-Glasnost event the Communist player has a sufficient Support Check modifier through the control of neighboring spaces he can Support Check the Democrat player out of “Ruse” space while limiting the +1VP Support Check penalty of the Eco-Glasnost event. If this is no longer possible because the Democrat player already has the upper hand in the Bulgarian worker triangle it can be triggered unless the Communist player is holding no other TST candidates.
