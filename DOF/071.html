Card Text: The Communist Player must randomly discard a card. If it is neutral or Democratic it takes place immediately.

   

Democrat play: Through counting cards on Turn 3 and Turn 7 the Democrat player gets information about which cards the Communist player is holding. These will often be worthwhile Democrat events that can justify the loss of the 3 Ops of the Kiss of Death card. Before Turn 7 it could however be worthwhile to keep it in the deck complicate the not allow the Communist to hold a card for 1 Turn unless the effects or Presidential Visit, Honecker and TST People’s Daily Denounces Protesters interfere.

 

   

Communist play: For the Communist playre having Kiss of Death card in his hand can cause problems by forcing him to trigger a problematic Democrat event. When Presidential Visit gets triggered the Communist player should hold the Kiss of Death card for Next Turn to make it a relatively harmless Action Round 7 play at the cost of limiting his flexibility. On the other hand with a full hand containing a Scoring card an Action Round 7 play of Kiss of Death can allow a 3 Ops Stealth Support Check, a Democrat space flip and/or taking Control of a space just before the Event triggers a “neutral” Scoring card with no downside. So just like Brought in for Questioning there is a to this event silver lining.
