Card Text: +1 VP for every Support Check by the Communist Player in Student or Intellectual spaces for the rest of the game.

   

Democrat play: The Democrat player should always trigger the Helsinki Final Act event to protect the Student spaces and the Intellectual spaces for the rest of the game. The Helsinki Final Act event will either be worth a lot of VPs if the Communist player gets into a desperate Support Check war with the Democrat player in Student spaces and Intellectual spaces or else it will force the Communist player to let the Democrat player take control of these cheap spaces for Presence and at least 4 extra Power Struggle cards and making 5 Leader cards playable if he get’s access to them. It is of course best triggered before the Intelligentsia event, the Jan Palach Week event, the Michnik event and the Fidesz event.

 

   

Communist play: Like the St. Nicholas Church event this is another Democrat event that the Communist player doesn’t want to trigger for the measly 1 Ops as the effect of the Helsinki Final Act event last for the rest of the game and therefore is worth way more than 1 Ops. The St. Nicholas Church card should be held at any cost until the risk of the Kiss of Death event and the Goodbye Lenin! event outweighs the chance of the event being triggered by the Reformer Rehabilitated event and the Dash for the West event. At that point the Helsinki Final Act should be discarded instead either through TSTing it, with the Common European Home event, with the General Strike event, as part of the People’s Daily Denounces Protesters bonus or possibly (though very unlikely) on the Inflationary Currency event.
