Chain of Events: Not part of a Chain

 

Canceled by / No effect after Resolution of: None

 

  
Card Text: If the Democrat holds Power in at least 2 countries, he may retreive any Scoring card from the discard pile and play it immediately as an event.

   

Democrat play: If there are countries in the Discard pile that are a slam dunk the Democrat player can immediately trigger the event for at least 4 VP in Final Scoring in addition to the normal effect of a Power Struggle win. If 2 contested countries are in the discard pile the Democrat player can build up his position in either taking the path of least resistance. The Democrat player should not forget to consider the card like Roundtable Talks, Peasant Parties Revolt, National Salvation Front, Yakovlev Counsels Gorbachev and The Crowd Turns Against Ceausescu and the possible events in the following Action Rounds effecting the country in question when considering how to go about taking Power. If the Democrat player’s positions is too weak in the countries he should take the -4VP hits in Final scoring and play Domino Theory for the 3 Ops.

 

   

Communist play: By Late Year the Democrat player should have met the precondition of Holding Power in at Least 2 countries. If he hasn’t or there are no Scoring cards in the discard pile it can be triggered as a harmless 3 Ops. Even if there is a Scoring card in the Discard pile it isn’t sure the Democrat would choose to play it. Often by Late Year the Communist player has completely secured some countries so that there is no hope for the Democrat to take Power or the Communist Player would get a sufficient amount of VP for holding on to Power an extra time to intimidate the Democrat player. Therefore it will often be trigger-able.
