Card Text: Remove all Democratic SPs from Czechoslovakia Elite and Bureaucratic Spaces.

   

Democrat play: If the Klaus and Komarek event hasn’t been triggered and the Democrat player hasn’t either tried to push for Czechoslovakia Control the Normalization event shouldn’t have any effect and is therefore easy to trigger for the Democrat player for 3 Ops as the Democrat player shouldn’t have any SPs in the “Plzen” space and the “Praha” space. As long as the number of Democrat SPs in the “Plzen” space and/or the “Praha” space are limited the 3 Ops of the the Normalization event card will mostly still outweigh the damage and there will be no need for the Democrat player to discard, TST or hold the Normalization card.

 

   

Communist play: It is very unlikely the Democrat player has any SPs in the “Plzen” space and unless the Klaus and Komarek event has been triggered also not in the “Praha” space either unless or the Democrat player is pushing to score Czechoslovakia Control. So mostly the Communist player will play the Normalization card for the 3 Ops. The Communist player can thereby keep the Normalization card in the deck as a threat to ‘protect’ the “Praha” space from attack through SP placement, Support Checks with Ops or events.
