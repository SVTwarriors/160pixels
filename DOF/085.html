Card Text: Advance 1 space on the Tiananmen Square Track.

   

Democrat play: Depending on when this card gets drawn Turn 8, Turn 9 or Turn 10 and depending on the Democrat’s player’s position in the TST both on the track and relative to the Communist player this event will be more valuable or not. In Late Year 2 Ops isn’t worth a whole lot and some of the last TST spot give a significant bonus to both the Democrat and the Communist player, so in Turn 8, Turn 9 It will often be played for the event. Be sure to first try to advance on the TST normally before using the Tank Column/Tank man event as the vice versa will require you roll 1 more to advance and don’t wait to long if it could effect either players “One time” or “Once per Turn” bonus.

 

   

Communist play: Drawn Turn 8 or Turn 9 it can help the Communist player get to the Structure removed TST spot and if he’s lucky get to the Protesters Massacred TST spot before the The Chinese Solution event gets played. Depending on the Communist player already having crossed the Structure removed spot or not he should play Tank Column/Tank man before or after a normal play on the TST. The Communist player should also time his advance considering the “One time” or “Once per Turn” bonus for both him and his opponent.
