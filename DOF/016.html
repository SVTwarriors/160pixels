Card Text: Place 3 Communist SPs in any Elite space(s), or remove all Democratic SPs from Elite spaces.

   

Democrat play: The Democrat player should trigger the Nomenklatura event as soon as possible allowing him to more easily and safely place SPs into Elite spaces. The main considerations Early Year being how the Nomenklatura event will effect the Poland Scoring event and the Hungary scoring event, if the Democrat player can be cut off from East Germany through the SP placement in the “Dresden” space and if the Nomenklatura event can help the Communist player gain the upper hand in the Bulgarian worker triangle.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player should nearly always play the Nomenklatura card for the 2 Ops to keep it's threat in the game as the Kremlin Coup! event triggering cannot always be relied on. If possible it should be held in Turn 2. The Democrat player will not want to place a lot of SPs into Elite spaces until the Nomenklatura card is removed from the game, protecting all Elite spaces from any significant Democrat SP placement before the Communist player has taken control of them also protecting them from invasion after the Malta Summit event, the Government Resigns event and the The Tyrant is Gone event. If the Democrat player triggers the event the Communist player should remember not to place any SPs in the “Szombathely” space until the Nagy Reburied card is removed from the game.
