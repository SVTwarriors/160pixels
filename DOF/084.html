Canceled by / No effect after Resolution of: None

 

  
Card Text: +5 VPs. Place sufficient Democratic SPs in any Minority space for control. Make 1 Support Check using the Ops value of this card. Requires play of The Baltic Way. Allows play of Kremlin Coup!

   

Democrat play: Being the third card in the Baltic event chain after Sajudis and The Baltic Way and being the prerequisite for Kremlin Coup! means this card will into always be played. When the Communist player gets the chance he will TST, discard or hold Sajudis and The Baltic Way. Even when the Communist players hand doesn’t allow him to avoid triggering the Breakaway Baltic Republics event the Kremlin Coup! event will still be in the deck or the Communist player’s hand. On the one hand Breakaway Baltic Republics does guarantee a successful roll on the Malta Summit event but on the other hand Kremlin Coup! makes the Malta Summit event unplayable. If Kremlin Coup! is no longer a concern the Democrat player should trigger the event.

 

   

Communist play: When (and only when) the Communist player is holding Kremlin Coup! and retaining Power in many countries with damaged Elite/Bureaucrat positions and holding other nasty Democrat events he can also not trigger it can be worth triggering the event for the 4 Ops the Communist player will at this point badly need. As Late Year +5 VP is a very very significant penalty and two 4 Ops Support Checks are also damaging the Communist player should in 99% of the cases TST, discard or hold.
