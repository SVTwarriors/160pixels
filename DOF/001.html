Card Text: Replace 1 Democratic SP in Poland with a Communist SP and make a Support Check in Poland using the Ops Value of this card.

   

Democrat play: The Legacy of Martial Law event is a relatively weak Communist event. If when the Democrat player triggers the event without giving the Communist player a Support Check roll with decent chance of success the damage can for the most part be fixed with the 2 Ops. Therefore it is often safely triggered by the Democrat Player as opposed to being discarded on the Tiananmen Square Track. A First consideration is for the Democrat player not to try to trigger the Legacy of Martial Law event when Perestroika is in effect as a 3 Ops Support check has a far higher chance of succeeding. Secondly the Democrat player should avoid entering Poland’s low stability spaces before the Legacy of Martial Law event has been triggered as they make an excellent target for it’s Support Check. This either through the placement of SP through Ops or through triggering of the Michnik event or the Intelligentsia event. When the Democrat player has already entered the “Polish Writers” space he should try to play the Helsinki Final Act event as soon as possible to dissuade both against “normal” Support Checks with Ops as against events like the Legacy of Martial Law if it is still in play. In this case the Democrat player should also refrain from playing 1 SP in the “Jagellonian University” space with Ops to take control of it as this SP will likely be flipped using Legacy of Martial Law event to give the Communist a +1 modifier on the event’s Support Check on the “Polish Writers” space.

 

   

Communist play: Unless there is a clear Support Check target for the Legacy of Martial Law event (with or without the Perestroika event's +1 Ops modifier) it is best to play this card for 2 Ops. This keeps the card in the deck with a 50% chance of ending up in the Democrat’s hand. For the Democrat the card will often be a wasted action round. However if Poland is still contested and the Communist player can get the Last Action Round of a Turn through the Honecker event or multiple Democrat discards (e.g. Deutsche Marks, Brought in for Questioning, Common European Home and to a lesser extent Inflationary Currency) , for the lack of better combos, the Legacy of Martial Law event can be used to set up an Action Round 1 Support Check for the next Turn.
