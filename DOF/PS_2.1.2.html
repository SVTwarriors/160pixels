2, Power Struggle

    2.1.2, Events Effecting Power Struggles

 

   

Below are all the cards that effect Powers Struggles and Scoring "directly". Further down are the cards that are effected by which faction holds Power. The cards are grouped accordingly.

Strategy cards that can themselves (temporarily) avoid Power Struggles:
   - Brought in for Questioning
   - Exit Visas
   - Samizdat

Strategy cards that can themselves directly trigger Power Struggles:
   - Kiss of Death
   - Domino Theory
   - New Year's Eve Party
Strategy cards that can themselves effect the Power Struggle draw:
   - Roundtable Talks
   - Kiss of Death
   - National Salvation Front

Strategy cards that can themselves effect the "main" Power Struggle:
   - Securitate & Army Backs the Revolution
   - Domino Theory
   - The Crowd Turns Against Ceausescu

Strategy cards that can themselves effect the Power Struggle Rolls and/or Scoring:
   - The Crowd Turns Against Ceausescu
   - Yakovlev councels Gorbachev
   - Kiss of Death
   - Peasant Parties Revolt
   - The Tyrant is Gone

Strategy cards that have events effected by total number of countries where the Communist player holds power:
   - Domino Theory
   - New Year's Eve Party

Strategy cards that have events effecting countries where the Communist player holds power:
   - Inflationary Currency
   - Central Committee Reshuffle
   - Workers Revolt
   - The Chinese Solution
   - Kremlin Coup!

Strategy cards that have events effecting countries where the Democrat player holds power:
   - Inflationary Currency
   - Workers Revolt
   - Shock Therapy
   - Social Democratic Platform Adopted
