Card Text: +1 Ops for all cards played by the Communist player for the rest of the turn.

   

Democrat play: The Perestroika card is easy to deal with by the Democrat player by simply having the Perestroika event trigger on the Democrat player’s 7th Action Round. This denies the Communist player any Bonus Ops unless the Honecker event is in effect and the Communist chooses to play this 8th Action Round.

 

   

Communist play: If the Communist player decides to trigger the Perestroika event it is of course best done in the Action Round 1 of a Turn. The more cards the Communist is holding that are Scoring cards and Communist events that he wants to trigger (that don't refer to the Ops value) the less interesting the Perestroika event becomes. Because the Perestroika card isn’t at all problematic for the Democrat player in his hand it should be considered to trigger the Perestroika event for 4+ bonus Ops. The Honecker event that could be triggered by either player should also be considered giving a possible extra Action Round to the Communist player and as a consequence an additional Bonus Ops. Please note that the Perestroika card does not mention a maximum of 4 Ops.
