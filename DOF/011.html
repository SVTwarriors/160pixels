Card Text: Remove 2 Democratic SPs from any intellectuals space.

   

Democrat play: It should be very easy for the Democrat player to repair the removal of 2 SP of the Dissidents Arrested event with 2 Ops. The Democrat player should be careful however not to lose access to an strategically important Intellectual space. The 1989 rulebook states "All SP markers must be placed with, or adjacent to, friendly SP markers that were in place before the first SP was placed.". So prior to triggering the event either have (a) SP(s) in the neighboring Student space or have at least 3 SPs in your important Intellectual space(s). 3 SPs has the added benefit of protecting against State Run Media event and the Scare Tactics Wild Card during the Power Struggle.

 

   

Communist play: Once the Helsinki Final Act event has been triggered the Communist player should try to deny the Democrat player access to the Student spaces and Intellectual spaces through the triggering of the Dissidents Arrested event and the State Run Media event and Control of their “gateway” spaces (e.g. the "Krakow" space, the "Budapest" space, the "Berlin" space, the "Praha" space, the "Sofia" space and the "Cluj-Napoca" space). This way the Communist can avoid losing VPs getting involved in expensive Support Check wars while denying the Democrat player the cheap Student spaces and Intellectual spaces and their associated leaders in Power Struggles. This is hard to do however because of the many Democratic events that give the Democrat player access (e.g. the Intelligentsia event, the Michnik event, the Jan Palach Week event and the Fidesz event).
