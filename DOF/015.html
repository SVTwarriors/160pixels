Honecker*

   

  

Player: C

  

Period: EY

  

Ops: 3

  

Type: 1x

  

Res: Imm.

  

Dur: Turn

  

Country: None

  

Space: None

  

SP: N

  

SC: N

  

VP: N

  

PS: N

  

TST: N

  

HS: Y

  

Chain of Events: Not part of a Chain

 

Canceled by / No effect after Resolution of: Modrow

 

  
Card Text: The Communist Player may take any non-scoring card in the discard pile and put it in his hand. The Communist Player may take one extra Action Round this turn. May not be played as an Event after Modrow.

   

Democrat play: The best moment to trigger the Honecker event as the Democrat player is Turn 1, 3 and 7, Action Round 1 while there can be nothing worth retrieving by the Communist player in the discard pile. The card will be removed form the game and the Communist player will still get the option to play a 8th Action Round but because the Communist player will often be wanting to hold on to one of the Democrat player’s cards (e.g. the Helsinki Final Act card, the St. Nicholas Church card, …) he will forgo it. Else the Democrat should always play it together with Common European Home, TST it or hold on to it as the Honecker event can open him up to a whole lot of pain otherwise.

 

   

Communist play: In Turn 2 the Communist player can use the Honecker event to hold two Democrat cards (e.g. the Helsinki Final Act card and the St. Nicholas Church card, …) as opposed to discarding them and having them return Turn 7 or sooner. Better still is the combination with Communist player’s SP placement/removal events (e.g. the State Run Media event, the Factory Party Cells event) by retrieving them out of the discard pile (or not) and then playing them on Action Round 8 in preparation of the next Turn's Action Round 1 Support Check. Another nice combo is triggering the Honecker event after an Action Round 1 play of Perestroika to draw the Gorbachev Charms the West card from the discard pile to again play it in Action Round 8 for two back-to-back Action Rounds. The best play of the Honecker event is Turn 10 as it can allow the Communist player to play the last game changing Action Round of the game. All-in-all one of the best Communist events of the game.
