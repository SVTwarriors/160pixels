5, Tiananmen Square Track

    5.2.6, Local PLA Support / Rural Divisions Summoned to Beijing

   

Bonus Text:“Make a free 2 Ops Support Check at End of Turn” (Please note: Events can modify the Ops value of this Bonus.)

 

 

 

 

   

Democrat Play: Because Middle Year the Democrat player has more “For the rest of the Turn” events that give him +1 Ops (e.g. Sinatra Doctrine, FRG embassies, ...) and Action Round 7 with event's or Ops he can break countries for a better modifier on the Local PLA Support Support Check this bonus is better for the Democrat player.

 

Democrat Score:

 

   

Communist Play: By Middle Year the Perestroika event is usually already out of the game, a 2 Ops Support Check by itself is weak and a triggered Helsinki Final Act usually be protecting it's natural targets, the Intellectual and Student spaces, from the Rural Divisions Summoned to Beijing bonus Support Check. The bonus Support Check is therefore often used by the Communist player to further solidify Control of countries where the Communist player already has the upper hand but where there is some stray Democratic SPs that were outbid for control of a Space earlier in the game. Turn 10 the Rural Divisions Summoned to Beijing bonus can be used by the COmmunist player to break the opponent's Czechoslovakia Control by Support Checking it's Intellectual or Student space for a -3 VP (Control->Domination) or even -5 VP (Control->Presence) Final scoring difference while taking the +1 VP hit from Helsinki Final Act if triggered.
