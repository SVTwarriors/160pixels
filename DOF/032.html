Card Text: Place 4 Communist SPs in Farmer space, no more than 2 per space.

   

Democrat play: Throughout the game the Peasant Parties card is mostly to be TSTed SPs by the Democrat player. The often Democrat player controlled Hungarian battleground Farmer space ”Szeged” can be broken mostly forcing the Democrat player to repair the damage with the 2 Ops. Otherwise Early Year it can This can help him get a stable foothold in the East of Poland for the Poland scoring event and Final scoring. Going into Middle Year the the Ops can also be used by the Communist player to significantly cheapen the taking control of the stable Farmer spaces of Middle Year and Late Year countries. These can thereby provide presence, access, deny presence or fortify the Communist players position in the Middle Year and Late Year countries.

 

   

Communist play: Before Turn 3 unless the Peasant Parties event could tip the scales in Poland or (less likely) in Hungary for scoring purposes the Communist player should play the Peasant Parties card for the 2 Ops keeping the card in the deck. Often the Democrat will end up triggering it later. In Early Year the SPs should most likely end up in the “Lublin” space and the “Bialystok” space if Poland is still being contested. This is done either to Support Check the “Gdansk” space (if the Solidarity Legalized event hasn't been triggered yet) or to form the defensive Lodz-Warszawa-Lublin triagle and/or the Warszawa-Bialystok-Lublin triangle or simply to get more Power Struggle cards for the upcoming Poland scoringevent. An added benefit being denying the Democrat player extra SPs from the Solidarity Legalized event by controlling the Farmer spaces. In Hungary it can be used to break the “Pecs” space forcing the Democrat player to react by fixing the damage and breaking his stride though he will likely find the 2 Ops. If the Communist player has the last Action Round of the Turn (e.g. through Democrat player discards or the Honecker event) it can also be use to set up a Support Check on the “Budapest” space at the start of next Turn. Middle Year in Bulgaria the west of the country around the “Sofia” space can be fortified to prevent the Democrat player taking over the West of Bulgaria through overwhelming Ops or The Union of Democratic Forces event. In Czechoslovakia the “Kosice” space can protect or threaten the “Drebecen” space in Hungary. In East Germany the “Erfurt” space can fortify East Germany against the Monday Demonstrations event or simply provide presence beyond the “Dresden” space. In Romania the Communist player can deny the Democrat player the access he gets through SP placement in the “Harghita/Covasna” space (e.g. the Laszlo Tokes event, the The Baltic Way event, the Sajudis event) by taking control of the “Targu Mures” space or taking control of the “Craiova” space to be able squeeze the “Timisoara” space.
