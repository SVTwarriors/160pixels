Card Text: Place sufficient SP in the Lutheran Church space and Leipzig for Democratic control. Then the Democratic Player may make 5 Support Checks in East Germany using the Ops value of this card. Requires play of St. Nicholas Church.

   

Democrat play: If "St. Nicolas Church" had been triggered and there are no unfavorable modifiers from events like Stand Fast, Grenztruppen or Prudence it should be triggered to either challenge the Communist's domination and/or achieve or strengthen Democrat domination of East Germany for eiher East Germany Scoring or Final Scoring. If "St. Nicolas Church" hasn't yet been triggered but could be in upcoming turns through Dash for the West, Reformer Rehabilitated, Goodbye Lenin!, Kiss of Death, Presidential Visit or simply a Democrat draw it should be held. The same applies to Turns with unfavorable modifiers.

 

   

Communist play: If St. Nicholas Church hasn't yet been triggered it is an easy 4 Ops. The likelihood of a St. Nicholas Church trigger later this game in combination with a Democrat trigger of The Monday Demonstrations event from the Discard pile using Dash for the West or "The Reformer Rehabilitated" or through a draw after a reshuffled should still be taken into account. The Monday Demonstrations should be held until Turn 7 and then TSTed unless the communist player is worried about Kiss of Death.
