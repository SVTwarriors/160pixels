Card Text: Place Democratic 3 SPs in any Catholic Church space.

   

Democrat play: Only in very rare cases when a “Catholic Church” space is being strongly contested for a tactical advantage, an event has given the Democrat player a -1 Ops modifier or access to the Czechoslovakian Church space was denied by the Communist player, should the Democrat consider triggering the Papal visit event. The Democrat player should mostly play the Papal visit card for 2 Ops and have the Communist player player trigger the Papal visit event for him later on.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player should quite easily be able to predict in which space the Democrat player will place the 3 SP of the Papal visit event. If the Democrat is in a tough spot in Hungary it will probably end up in the Hungarian “Catholic Church” space. If Middle Year is approaching or the Communist player made an early move Czechoslovakia the SP will often end up in the Czechoslovakian “Catholic Church” space and if the the Democrat player lost control of the Polish “Catholic Church” space it will very likely end up in Poland to fix the damage. Often the democrat won't place a lot of SPs in the “Catholic Church” spaces in anticipation of this event. This can in rare cases where the Communist player has a lot of Ops for SPs allow him to play into these stable but expensive spaces trying to outbid the Democrat player. If the Communist player has denied the Democrat player access to Czechoslovakia he should hold the Papal visit card, TST it or play it with the Common European Home event whenever possible until he has secured the region.
