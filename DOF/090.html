Card Text: +1 VP. Place 4 SPs in Czechoslovakia. Then the Democratic Player may make Support Checks in Czechoslovakia using the Ops value of this card if the Czech Writers space is Democratically controlled.

   

Democrat play: This event is very powerful and should be triggered by the Democrat player even if he probably already has a strong position in Czechoslovakia. If Late Year the Democrat’s position is unusually weak this event together with Public Against Violence will allow him to break into the Brno-Ostrava-Bratislava worker triangle. If Normalization is out of the game and the Communist player’s presence in Czechoslovakia is limited to the Plzen-Praha-Ceske Budejovice triangle the Democrat can try to take Praha for Democrat control of Czechoslovakia. The Democrat should try take control of the Czechoslovakian Intellectual space prior to playing the event so he doesn’t have to waste the Civic Forum events SPs on taking control of it.

 

   

Communist play: Unless the Communist player has 3 SPs in the Czechoslovakia Intellectual space making this event a +1VP wasted Action Round he should TST it. Getting 3SPs in the Czechoslovakian Intellectual space will often require some luck, two Support Checks in Intellectual and Student spaces and therefore an extra 2VP penalty because of Helsinki Final Act. In most cases The effort required to repair the damage done by the Civic Forum event will far exceed the 4 Ops.
