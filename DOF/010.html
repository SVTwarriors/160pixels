Canceled by / No effect after Resolution of: The Tyrant is Gone

 

  
Card Text: Place 4 Communist SPs in Worker or Farmer spaces in Romania, no more than 2 per space. Has no effect after The Tyrant is Gone.

   

Democrat play: Early Year the Democrat player should play this for the Ops and not worry too much about the Late Year country Romania. After Turn 2 if the Communist player hasn't yet placed SPs into the "Varna" space or the "Burgas" space the Democrat player could attempt to gain the upper hand in the Bulgarian Worker traingle by Controling the "Ruse" space to subsequently take Control of the "Varna" space or the "Burgas" space before triggering the Cult of Personality event. When triggered Early Year an Middle Year some of the Cult of Personality event's SPs would probably be landing in the "Constanta" space to access/protect/threaten the "Varna" space and hinder the Democrat player when entering Romania later on in the game.

 

   

Communist play: Only after Turn 7 should the Communist player consider playing the Cult of Personality card for the event as the focus shifts to Romania and before The Tyrant is Gone can trigger. Early Year the focus should be on Poland and Hungary and considering the Communist player already has good access in Romania the Communist player should let the Democrat player trigger the Cult of Personality event for him. When the Democrat player does trigger the Cult of Personality event the Communist player should place two SP in the "Constanta" space to access/protect/threaten the "Varna" space. If the Communist player is able to control and hold either the "Varna" space or the "Burgas" space the Democrat player will be in trouble in Bulgaria as he can be squeezed out of the Bulgarian worker triangle resulting in an early Communist player lead in both Balkan countries.
