Card Text: Remove 4 Communist SPs from the Romanian Elite Space. The Democrat announces where the Ceausecus will flee to, choose a space in Romania with no Democratic SPs. Requires play of The Crowd turns Against Ceausescu.

   

Democrat play: If The Crowd Turns Against Ceausescu has already been triggered during Romania scoring The Tyrant is Gone triggers automatically. Because Romania scoring isn’t a sure thing the Democrat shouldn’t trigger The Tyrant is Gone event prematurely. When Romania scoring has caused The Crowd Turns Against Ceausescu to trigger the Democrat player can play the The Tyrant is Gone event and if he has access to them make the Ceausescus flee to the low stability Student or Intellectual space to easily take control and grab the 2 VPs. Denying the Communist a Battleground and a Control should mean an other 3 VP. This stacks up well against 2 Ops. Having the Government Resigns in the game to dissuade the Communist from re-investing in the Elite space and being confident Kremlin Coup! won’t help.

 

   

Communist play: If The Crowd Turns Against Ceausescu isn’t going to trigger at the start of Romania scoring it is a free 2 Ops. If the event will or could trigger much depends on if the Romanian Student Space and Intellectual space are Democrat controlled. Turn 10 when holding The Tyrant is Gone together with events that can repair the damage to the Romanian Elite space like Kremlin Coup!, Elena or Nomenklatura the event can depending the situation even mean -2VPs if the Ceausescus aren’t captured by the end of the game. But the Communist player needs to be aware of Democrat events like Government Resigns and Malta Summit that can disrupt this attempt.
