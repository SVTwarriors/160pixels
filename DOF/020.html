Card Text: The Democrat gives the Communist his highest Ops value non-scoring card. If it is a Communist Event that is still playable, it takes place immediately; otherwise use the Ops value of the card" without triggering the Event.

   

Democrat play: The Deutsche Marks event is best played in the 7th Action Round by the Democrat player together with the Stasi card without effect or any other Communist 1 Ops card (e.g. the The Wall card, the Tear Gas card…) with minimal effect. This way the Deutsche Marks event is triggered and the only Communist 4 Ops card is removed from the game. Else the Deutsche Marks event can be triggered on his last Action Round when the Democrat player already has a reduced hand size. As a last resort it can be held into the next Turn until one of the previously mentioned options manifests itself or played with a higher Ops Communist card with an event that isn’t problematic or a low Ops Democrat card with an event that doesn't need triggering.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player will mostly play the Deutsche Marks card for it's 4 Ops unless Turn 3 or Turn 7 Action Round 1 he knows what the outcome of triggering the event will be through the counting of 4 Ops cards. There are no other 4 Ops Communist events so whenever the Democrat player is holding a 4 Ops card he will be forced to give either a Neutral or Democrat card. This still allows the Communist player to play 4 Ops and as a bonus reduces the Democrat player's hand size and denies the Democrat that same 4 Ops card. Turn 2 depending on the number of Early Year 4 Ops cards that have yet to be played and are in the Communist player's own hand the Deutsche Marks event can be triggered on an educated guess. There is no real point to keeping the Deutsche Marks card in the game as it mostly doesn't form a problem in the Democrat's hand, quite the opposite often. When the Communist isn't sure the Democrat is holding a 4 card there are enough 3 Ops Communist cards that the Democrat player can and will gladly give for the Communist player to trigger their relatively weak event. Otherwise an expensive option is the use of the Deutsche Marks event in combination with other cards (e.g. the Brought in for Questioning card, the Common European Home card and to a lesser extent the Inflationary Currency card) for sufficient reduction of the Democrat player's hand size as to effect his ability to play his Action Round 7.
