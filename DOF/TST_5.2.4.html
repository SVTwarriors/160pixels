5, Tiananmen Square Track

    5.2.4, Student Hunger Strike / Students Arrested

   

Bonus Text:“Remove 2 Opponent Support Points (One Time)”

 

 

 

 

   

Democrat Play: A rather insignificant Bonus for the Democrat player. While the damage done by the "One Time Use" Student Hunger Strike bonus can normally be fixed quite easily by the Communist player, it will force him to spend his already limited “free” Ops. Annoying the opponent by disrupting his plans.

 

Democrat Score:

 

   

Communist Play: A relatively weak Bonus also for the Communist player that sometimes can deny access to the Democrat player however, after he has played the Intelligentsia event for example. Aside from this the Students Arrested bonus is likely no more than a minor nuisance for the Democrat player unless the Communist player is able to use it on "the" last Action Round of a Turn by triggering the Honecker event or forcing the Democrat to discard sufficient cards.
