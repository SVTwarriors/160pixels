Player: D

  

Period: MY

  

Ops: 3

  

Type: 1x

  

Res: Next PS

  

Dur: PIFOP

  

Country: None

  

Space: None

  

SP: N

  

SC: N

  

VP: N

  

PS: Y

  

TST: N

  

HS: N

  

Chain of Events: Not part of a Chain

 

Canceled by / No effect after Resolution of: None

 

  
Card Text: Place in front of the Democrat. In the next Power Struggle if the Democrat controls a Farmer space, he draws 1 Power Struggle card at random from the Communist hand and places it in his own. Then remove this card from the game

   

Democrat play: The Democrat should play Peasant Parties Revolt for the 3 Ops unless here is certain the next Power Struggle be a crucial one in a country with a Democrat Controlled Farmer space.

 

   

Communist play: Normally the Peasant Parties Revolt event should be nothing for the Communist player to worry about. The event is pretty weak and the Communist player will be needing the Ops desperately. When possible he should try to trigger the event when he suspects the next Power Struggle will be held in a country where the Democrat doesn’t control a Farmer space.
