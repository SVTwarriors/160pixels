5, Tiananmen Square Track

    5.2.8, Political Pluralism / Most Favored Nations Trade Status Awarded

   

Bonus Text:“Play a Card For Both Ops and Event Once Per Turn”

 

 

 

 

   

Democrat Play: This is nearly impossible (and very rare) for the Democrat to achieve requiring the "Tank Column/Tank Man" event and major Ops sacrifice and a lot of lucky rolls. But if achieved this can be a killer Bonus as it allows the Democrat to set up two 3-4 Ops Support Checks using the Democrat card's event (e.g. Soviet Troop Withdrwals, Union of Democratic Forces, Monday Demonstrations, Civic Forum, Walesa). In Turn 10 Action Round 7 this can result in a massive VP swing for the Final Scoring. It can also be used to to score a country with Domino Theory after changing the facts on the ground with the 3 Ops. Clearly there are many other possible combos.

 

Democrat Score:

 

   

Communist Play: A good event but not nearly as good as it is for the Democrat player as the best Communist cards to use this Bonus with are only 3 Ops cards. Then again a 5 on TST being so easy to achieve it would be wasteful not to take the Bonus, as reaching the end of the TST track doesn't have a drawback as you can still play cards on it you want to discard.
