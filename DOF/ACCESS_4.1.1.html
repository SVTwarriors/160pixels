4, Access denial and Lockout

    4.1.1, Hungary

 

   

To attempt to deny the Democrat player access to the Hungarian Budapest-Tatabanya-Miskolc worker triangle the communist need to hold both a card with a remove democratic SP event (e.g. State Run Media, Gorbachev Charms the West) and Hungarian Democratic Forum to avoid surprises. The Democrat player may also not have placed any initial influence in Hungary. Turn 1 the Communist player should also examine his hand for other cards. Holding cards that can give the Democratic player presence through the event (e.g. Fidesz, Intelligentsia (if not holding a playable 4 Ops card), Papal visit) or a Support Check (e.g. 4Ops cards, Gorbachev Charms the West, Consumerism) and cards that help the Communist player regain access to Budapest-Tatabanya-Miskolc worker triangle (if he loses it after a Support Check) (e.g. Nagy Reburied, Pozsgay Defends the Revolution, Peasant Parties, Apparatchiks) can help the Communist player decide how he wants to open Turn 1 – Action Round 1.

The odds for Democrat presence after +0 Support Check(s) on Miskolc
- 4 Ops card (if both SC rolls are used on Berlin if possible) = ±53%
- The Gorbachev Charms the West event (1SP left after removal) = ±50%
- The Consumerism event (1SP left after removal) = ±33%
- 3 Ops card (if both SC rolls are used on Berlin if possible) = ±31%

Holding: SRM + HDF
Step 1: The Communist player places 2 SP Initial influence in Miskolc
Step 2: The Communist player plays the State Run Media event (remove 1 SP from Budapest, Szeged, and Szekefehervar each. The last removed SP can be used to deny the Democrat Player access to Czechoslovakia, East Germany (if possible) or disrupt Poland forcing the Democrat to choose between a Support Check and repairing the damage)

Step 3: The Democrat player Support Checks

Holding: GCTW + HDF + FIDESZ
Step 1: The Communist player places 2 SP Initial influence in Miskolc
Step 2: The Communist player plays the Gorbachev Charms the West event (remove 1 SP from Szekefehervar and 1 SP from Szeged and Support Check Budapest)
Step 3: The Democrat takes Gyor (or decides to Support Check)
Step 4: The Communist takes control of Budapest and places SPs in Miskolc


Holding: SRM + HDF + FIDESZ
Step 1: The Communist player places 1 SP Initial influence in Kosice (optional: and 1 SP Initial influence in Pecs for added flexibility in reacting to the Democrat player entering with Intelligentsia without getting into a Support Check war by playing 4 Ops in Szeged.)
Step 2: The Communist player plays the State Run Media event (remove 1 SP from Budapest, Szeged, Szekesfehervar and the Hungarian Catholic Church each.)

Step 3: The Democrat player plays Papal visit
Step 4: The Communist player takes Debrecen and should be able to secure the Hungarian worker triangle.
