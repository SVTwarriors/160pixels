Card Text: Place 3 Communist SPs in any one country where the Communist still holds Power.

   

Democrat play: Unless East Germany is no longer contested or the St. Nicholas Church event hasn’t been triggered yet the Democrat player shouldn't play the “We are the people!” card for the Event. If Early Year the St. Nicholas Church event was triggered the Democrat player should have 4 SPs on the “Lutheran Church” space in East Germany to spread around. These 4 SPs will later be replenished by the The Monday Demonstrations event if it triggers. So the best order for the events to trigger for the Democrat player is the St. Nicholas Church event → the “We are the people!” event → the The Monday Demonstrations event. If the Communist player is holding on to the The Monday Demonstrations card or it is in the discard pile the points could be placed in the Leipzig space if the Ops value of the other cards in the Democrat player’s hand allows him to wrestle away control of the Leipzig space. Else the Democrat should probably not move SPs to the “Leipzig” space. The Democrat player should when moving SPs be sure East Germany doesn’t get scored while he doesn’t have Presence through the “Lutheran Church” space though. If the Helsinki Final Act act is active and the Democrat player doesn’t yet have access to the East German Intellectual and Student spaces he could move some SPs there.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist player shouldn’t trigger unless St. Nicholas Church hasn’t been played yet and he controls and preferably is overprotecting the “Leipzig” space or the Democrat player has been denied access to the “Leipzig” space. Otherwise it should be TSTed when possible.
