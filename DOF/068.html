Card Text: Remove 2 Communist SPs and add 2 Democratic SPs in Praha.

   

Democrat play: The Democrat player should normally trigger the Klaus and Komarek event only after the Normalization event has been triggered or is unlikely to be triggered for the rest of the game. Following through on Klaus and Komarek with sufficient Ops or Civic Forum can mean a 4 VP difference for Final Scoring.

 

   

Communist play: The Communist will want to trigger this event before Normalization. He will need to have a 4 SP lead in the Praha space and then use the 3 Ops of Klaus and Komarek to shore it up after the event takes place but is better than risking to lose the Plzen-Praha-Ceske Budejovice triangle which usually gives the Communist player Presence and denies the Democrat player Control.
