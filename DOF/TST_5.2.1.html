5, Tiananmen Square Track

    5.2.1, Reformer Memorialized / Reformer Discredited

   

Bonus Text:“+1 Modifier for Tiananmen Square Die Rolls”

 

 

 

 

   

Democrat Play: For the Democrat player it should be relatively easy to advance to the Reformer Memorialized TST spot in the beginning of the game. This is because Early Year the Democrat player doesn't really have 1 Ops TST candidates and can therefore easily clear the low 5 hurdle of this spot with 2 Ops or higher. In contrast the Communist will have more difficulty to get to the Reformer Discredited spot. This means most games the Democrat player is more likely to enjoy this "+1 Modifier" Bonus. Because the Democrat's first two TST spots have a low hurdle the +1 Modifier of this Bonus can sometimes be overkill for the Democrat player however. When the Bonus does increase the Democrat player's chances to advance on the TST he should take advantage of it before the Communist player catches up, as he is only one (good) roll away from getting to the Reformer Discredited spot.

 

Democrat Score:

 

   

Communist Play: Often the Communist player will get behind on the TST early in the game because he initially has to clear a higher 6 hurdle than the Democrat player to advance on the two first TST spots. Moreover unlike the Democrat he is sometimes forced to do so with a 1 Ops Democrat card with a meager 33.33% chance to advance on his first TST roll. This while the Democrat player will in all likelihood get the Reformer Memorialized Bonus his first Turn and the Students Rally Bonus the Turn after further widening the gap. Some games the Democrat player TSTs a 2 Ops Communist card and misses. If the Communist player is then able to TST a 3 Ops Democrat card on his first roll (e.g. General Strike, Round Table Talks, Hungarian Democratic Forum, …). This can allow the Communist player to get ahead of his opponent if the Democrat player has an "unlucky" first TST roll. The next Turn, before the Democrat player TSTs, making use of the Reformer Discredited the Communist player can then attempt to pull further ahead on the TST. If successful this evens the odds by reducing the Communist player's handicap on the TST at the start of the game. This in turn increases the Communist player's chances to take the two “One Time” Bonuses, trigger the Reformer Rehabilitated and The Chinese Solution events and reach/enjoy the later TST bonuses.
